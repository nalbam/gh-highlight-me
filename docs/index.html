Features

🎯

Auto-detection

Automatically detects your GitHub username when you're logged in. No manual setup needed.

⚡

Custom Identifiers

Add team names, other usernames, or keywords you want to track. Highlight what matters to you.

🔄

SPA Support

Works seamlessly with GitHub's PJAX navigation. Highlights persist as you browse.

🎨

Customizable Colors

Choose your own colors for username and each identifier. Personalize your highlighting experience.

🌓

Dark Mode

Full support for GitHub's dark theme. Looks great in any color mode.

🔒

Privacy Focused

No data collection. No external requests. Everything stays local on your device.
